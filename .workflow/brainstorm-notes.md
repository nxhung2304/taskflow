# TaskFlow App Ideas

1. **Personal Productivity Dashboard** - Comprehensive task management with time tracking, priority levels, and personal analytics.

2. **Team Collaboration Hub** - Multi-user task assignment with role-based permissions, comments, and real-time notifications.

3. **Habit Tracker & Goal Setting** - Long-term goal breakdown with streak tracking and progress visualization.

4. **Freelancer Project Manager** - Client management with project timelines, invoice tracking, and hour logging.

5. **Learning Path Organizer** - Structured course/skill tracking with resources and progress markers.

6. **Home Maintenance Scheduler** - Recurring household tasks with seasonal reminders and expense tracking.

7. **Event Planning Coordinator** - Multi-stage event tasklists with vendor management and budget tracking.

8. **Health & Wellness Routine** - Medication reminders, exercise scheduling, and symptom logging.

9. **Business Process Automation** - Workflow templates with approval chains and document management.

10. **Study Group Organizer** - Shared academic task lists with resources and collaborative features.
